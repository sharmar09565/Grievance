Policies & Guidelines

Confidentiality Assured

The identity of the complaint is kept strictly confidential. Grievance details are accessible only to authorized committee members.

False Complaints Discovered

False, misleading complaints are strictly prohibited. If a complaint is proven false, disciplinary action will be taken against the complainant.

Resolution Timeframe: 7–10 Working Days

Every grievance will be reviewed within 7–10 working days. Complex cases may take longer; the complainant will be informed.

UGC & AICTE Norms Followed

The grievance process follows UGC (Grievance Redressal of Students) Regulations and AICTE norms and institutional guidelines.
