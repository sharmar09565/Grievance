About Grievance Cell

The Grievance Redressal Portal is designed to effectively address genuine concerns and complaints raised by Students, Faculty, and other Stakeholders. It provides a structured mechanism to receive, examine, and resolve various types of grievances in a fair and timely manner. Stakeholders are encouraged to share their concerns through the suggestion boxes installed at various locations across the campus. Additionally, they may directly approach members of the Grievance Redressal Cell or any faculty member they feel comfortable with, ensuring open communication and transparency in the grievance resolution process.

Students, Faculty, and other Stakeholders are advised to recognize that lodging a complaint is a serious matter and should be done responsibly and in good faith. At the same time, the institution assures all complainants that every grievance submitted will be handled with utmost sensitivity, impartiality, and strict confidentiality, ensuring a safe and respectful grievance redressal process.
